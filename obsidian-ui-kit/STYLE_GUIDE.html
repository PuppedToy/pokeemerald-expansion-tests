PRINCIPLES & COLOR

  • Hard edges only. border-radius is always 0.
  • Offset shadows = depth. Solid, non-blurred, down-right (e.g. 4px 4px 0 #04080f). Lift on hover, sink on press.
  • Navy surfaces, orange action. Orange = primary CTA, cyan = secondary/interactive. Accents pop against the navy.
  • Pixel type, two roles. Press Start 2P for chrome, VT323 for reading.
  • Snappy, stepped motion. 0.08s transitions; steps() spinners.
