# Setup Sweepers — Smogon Gen5-9 Research

Pokemon that punched above their BST via setup moves.

---

## Quiver Dance Sweepers

### Volcarona (BST 550, BW+ OU)
- **Stats:** 85/60/65/135/105/100
- **Combo:** Quiver Dance + Fire Blast + Bug Buzz + Giga Drain
- **Ability:** Flame Body (or Swarm)
- **Item:** Life Orb / Lum Berry
- **Why it works:** Quiver Dance gives +1 SpA, SpD, Spe in one move. At +1 it outspeeds almost everything and hits at 202 SpA effective. Just one turn of setup and it's nearly unstoppable. Giga Drain patches Water weakness slightly.
- **Pattern:** Quiver Dance = triple-stat setup. Single turn transforms a mid-bulk mon into an untouchable sweeper.

### Ribombee (BST 464, SM OU briefly)
- **Combo:** Quiver Dance + Moonblast + Bug Buzz + Hidden Power Ground
- **Ability:** Shield Dust / Sweet Veil
- **Why it works:** Same pattern as Volcarona but faster base speed (124). Frail but outspeeds everything after +1.

## Dragon Dance Sweepers

### Gyarados (BST 540, BW+ OU)
- **Stats:** 95/125/79/60/100/81
- **Combo:** Dragon Dance + Waterfall + Ice Fang + Earthquake
- **Ability:** Moxie (BW2+) or Intimidate
- **Item:** Lum Berry / Life Orb
- **Why it works:** Moxie raises Attack on each KO — snowballs out of control. DD+Moxie means each kill gets stronger AND faster. Waterfall is reliable STAB, Ice Fang hits Dragons/Grass, EQ handles Electrics.
- **Pattern:** Dragon Dance + Moxie = exponential power gain per kill.

### Haxorus (BST 540, BW OU)
- **Stats:** 76/147/90/60/70/97
- **Combo:** Dragon Dance + Outrage + Earthquake + X-Scissor
- **Ability:** Mold Breaker
- **Item:** Lum Berry
- **Why it works:** Mold Breaker ignores Levitate (hits Rotom-W with EQ, hits Bronzong with EQ). Outrage after DD is almost impossible to wall. Lum Berry cures Outrage confusion.
- **Pattern:** Mold Breaker + DD = bypasses the two most common immunities (Levitate, Wonder Guard).

### Dragonite (BST 600, BW OU — just over threshold but worth noting)
- **Combo:** Dragon Dance + ExtremeSpeed + Outrage + Fire Punch
- **Ability:** Multiscale
- **Why it works:** Multiscale halves damage at full HP — guaranteed safe setup turn. ExtremeSpeed after DD revenge-kills faster threats.
- **Pattern:** Multiscale = free setup turn at full HP.

## Swords Dance Sweepers

### Scizor (BST 430, BW+ OU)
- **Stats:** 70/130/100/55/80/65
- **Combo:** Swords Dance + Bullet Punch + Bug Bite + Superpower
- **Ability:** Technician
- **Item:** Life Orb / Choice Band
- **Why it works:** Technician boosts Bullet Punch from 40 to 60 BP (then STAB = 90 effective). After SD, priority Bullet Punch at +2 demolishes. SD+priority means no need for speed — sweeps even Choice Scarf users.
- **Pattern:** Technician + priority + SD = sweeper that doesn't need speed at all.

### Lucario (BST 525, BW/XY OU)
- **Stats:** 70/110/70/115/70/90
- **Combo:** Swords Dance + ExtremeSpeed + Close Combat + Ice Punch
- **Ability:** Justified / Inner Focus
- **Item:** Life Orb
- **Why it works:** ExtremeSpeed at +2 hits extremely hard with priority. Justified raises Atk when hit by Dark moves — opponents can't safely use Knock Off. CC handles most walls.
- **Pattern:** SD + ExtremeSpeed = sweeper immune to priority revenge kills.

### Mega Mawile (BST 480, XY/ORAS Uber → then banned)
- **Stats:** 50/105/125/55/95/50 → Mega: 50/105+Huge Power/125/55/95/50
- **Combo:** Swords Dance + Play Rough + Iron Head + Sucker Punch
- **Ability:** Huge Power (doubles Attack → effectively 210 base Atk)
- **Item:** Mawilite
- **Why it works:** Huge Power makes 105 Atk effectively 210. After SD that's +2 with 210 base. Sucker Punch provides priority. Play Rough/Iron Head are strong STABs.
- **Pattern:** Huge Power + SD = Attack stat that no wall can handle.

### Excadrill (BST 508, BW OU/Uber)
- **Stats:** 110/135/60/50/65/88
- **Combo:** Swords Dance + Earthquake + Iron Head + Rock Slide
- **Ability:** Sand Rush (doubles speed in sand) or Sand Force
- **Item:** Air Balloon / Life Orb
- **Why it works:** In sand, Sand Rush doubles speed → 176 effective speed outspeeds everything. SD+Sand Rush = both damage and speed covered in one setup.
- **Pattern:** Weather + speed doubling ability + SD = dominant in weather teams.

## Nasty Plot Sweepers

### Thundurus (BST 580, BW OU)
- **Stats:** 79/115/70/125/80/111
- **Combo:** Nasty Plot + Thunderbolt + Focus Blast + Hidden Power Ice
- **Ability:** Prankster
- **Item:** Life Orb
- **Why it works:** Prankster gives priority to Thunder Wave and Taunt — can paralyze or prevent opponent setup before NP. After +2 SpA, hits everything neutrally or super-effectively.
- **Pattern:** Prankster + setup = opponent can't stop you setting up (priority taunt/para).

### Gengar (BST 500, multiple gens OU)
- **Stats:** 60/65/60/130/75/110
- **Combo:** Nasty Plot + Shadow Ball + Focus Blast + Thunderbolt/HP Fire
- **Ability:** Levitate
- **Item:** Life Orb / Black Sludge
- **Why it works:** Levitate gives Ground immunity (safe vs Excadrill/Hippowdon). After NP at +2 SpA=260, walls don't exist. Spinblocks for team.
- **Pattern:** NP + immunity ability = free setup against grounded threats.

### Gholdengo (BST 550, SV OU)
- **Stats:** 87/60/95/133/91/84
- **Combo:** Nasty Plot + Make It Rain + Shadow Ball + Focus Blast/Thunderbolt
- **Ability:** Good as Gold (immune to ALL status moves — cannot be Taunted, T-Waved, etc.)
- **Item:** Life Orb / Choice Specs
- **Why it works:** Good as Gold makes it immune to Taunt (can't stop NP), Thunder Wave (can't slow it down), Will-O-Wisp (can't burn it). After NP, Steel/Ghost coverage is nearly unresisted.
- **Pattern:** Good as Gold + NP = setup that literally cannot be stopped by status/taunt.

## Shell Smash Sweepers

### Cloyster (BST 525, BW OU)
- **Stats:** 50/95/180/85/45/70
- **Combo:** Shell Smash + Icicle Spear + Rock Blast + Razor Shell
- **Ability:** Skill Link
- **Item:** Focus Sash / White Herb
- **Why it works:** Shell Smash gives +2 Atk/SpA/Spe at -1 Def/SpDef. Skill Link makes multi-hit moves always land 5 times — Icicle Spear = 125 BP, Rock Blast = 125 BP. White Herb restores the defense drop.
- **Pattern:** Skill Link + Shell Smash + multi-hit = guaranteed 125 BP moves that break Sash/Substitute.

### Barbaracle (BST 490, XY OU fringe)
- **Combo:** Shell Smash + Razor Shell + Stone Edge + Cross Chop
- **Ability:** Tough Claws (boosts contact moves by 1.3x)
- **Why it works:** Tough Claws + Shell Smash + strong contact moves. Stone Edge handles Flying/Ice types.

## Calm Mind Sweepers

### Reuniclus (BST 490, BW OU)
- **Stats:** 110/65/75/125/85/30
- **Combo:** Calm Mind + Psyshock + Focus Blast + Shadow Ball / Recover
- **Ability:** Magic Guard
- **Item:** Life Orb
- **Why it works:** Magic Guard = no Life Orb recoil, no residual damage. Very slow but bulky. Under Trick Room, 30 speed becomes an asset. After CM, its bulk + CM SpDef boost makes it nearly unkillable by special attackers. Psyshock hits Blissey/Chansey on Defense stat (bypasses special bulk).
- **Pattern:** Magic Guard + Life Orb = best offensive item with zero drawback. Psyshock + CM = counters special walls.

### Suicune (BST 580, BW+ OU)
- **Combo:** Calm Mind + Scald + Ice Beam + Substitute / Rest
- **Ability:** Pressure
- **Item:** Leftovers
- **Why it works:** CM stacks SpA and SpD. Pressure depletes opponent PP. Scald has 30% burn chance. After several CMs it's nearly impossible to break. Rest + CM SpDef = full recovery while still threatening offensively.
- **Pattern:** CM + Pressure + Scald = stall that becomes an offensive threat over time.

## Special Setups

### Azumarill (BST 420, XY+ OU)
- **Stats:** 100/50/80/60/80/50 — but Huge Power doubles ATK → effectively 100 ATK
- **Combo:** Belly Drum + Aqua Jet + Play Rough + Knock Off
- **Ability:** Huge Power
- **Item:** Sitrus Berry (restores HP after Belly Drum halves it)
- **Why it works:** Belly Drum = +6 Atk at cost of 50% HP. Huge Power doubles base attack, so after BD it's at +6 with effectively 200 base Atk. Aqua Jet priority means speed irrelevant. Sitrus Berry restores the HP lost from BD.
- **Pattern:** Belly Drum + Huge Power + priority = one-turn nuke setup.

### Hawlucha (BST 500, XY OU)
- **Stats:** 78/92/75/74/63/118
- **Combo:** Swords Dance + Acrobatics + High Jump Kick + Stone Edge
- **Ability:** Unburden
- **Item:** Power Herb (consumed for instant Sky Attack) or Flying Gem
- **Why it works:** Unburden doubles speed when held item is consumed. Fast base 118 → effectively 236 after Unburden. After SD+Unburden it outspeeds Choice Scarf users. Acrobatics doubles BP when no item held.
- **Pattern:** Unburden + consumable item + SD = instantaneous fastest sweeper in the tier.

### Kingambit (BST 550, SV OU)
- **Stats:** 100/135/120/60/85/50
- **Combo:** Swords Dance + Kowtow Cleave + Sucker Punch + Iron Head
- **Ability:** Supreme Overlord (raises Atk 10% per fainted teammate, up to 50%)
- **Item:** Leftovers / Black Glasses
- **Why it works:** Supreme Overlord means late-game (when team is depleted) it hits at ~135+50% = ~200 effective Atk. Kowtow Cleave never misses. Sucker Punch is priority. After SD with Supreme Overlord stacks, walls crumble.
- **Pattern:** Supreme Overlord + SD + priority = exponentially stronger as game progresses.

### Annihilape (BST 535, SV OU)
- **Stats:** 110/115/80/50/90/90
- **Combo:** Rage Fist + Bulk Up + Rest / Drain Punch + Ghost/Fighting coverage
- **Ability:** Defiant (raises Atk +2 when stats lowered by opponent)
- **Item:** Leftovers
- **Why it works:** Rage Fist gains +50 BP for each time Annihilape has been hit (persists through fainting and recalling). Can accumulate to massive BP. Defiant punishes Intimidate/Parting Shot. Bulk Up stacks Defense and Atk.
- **Pattern:** Accumulating power move + Bulk Up = gets stronger by taking damage.

## Speed Boost Setup

### Blaziken (BST 530, XY Uber — banned from OU)
- **Stats:** 80/120/70/110/70/80
- **Combo:** Swords Dance + High Jump Kick + Flare Blitz + Protect
- **Ability:** Speed Boost
- **Item:** Life Orb
- **Why it works:** Speed Boost gives +1 Spe each turn. Protect wastes opponent turn while getting free Speed boost. After 1-2 turns, outspeeds everything while at +2 Atk. HJK at +2 OHKO/2HKO everything.
- **Pattern:** Speed Boost + Protect + SD = guaranteed setup with no counterplay.

### Scolipede (BST 485, BW/XY OU fringe — mainly Baton Pass)
- **Combo:** Speed Boost + Baton Pass + Swords Dance / Substitute
- **Ability:** Speed Boost
- **Why it works:** Passes accumulated Speed and Attack boosts to a sweeper. Fastest Baton Pass user at 112 speed.

## Mega-specific Setup

### Mega Medicham (BST 510, XY OU)
- **Stats:** 60/100/75/55/75/100 → Pure Power doubles Atk → effectively 200 Atk
- **Combo:** High Jump Kick + Zen Headbutt + Bullet Punch + Ice Punch
- **Ability:** Pure Power
- **Why it works:** Pure Power doubles Attack stat, making 100 base effectively 200. HJK with STAB at effectively 200 base Atk is one of the strongest unboosted attacks in the game. Ice Punch handles Dragons/Flyers.
- **Pattern:** Pure Power = every physical move is doubled. No setup needed.
