# Stall / Walls — Smogon Gen5-9 Research

Pokemon whose defensive combos made them tier fixtures despite mediocre offense.

---

## Poison Heal + Substitute Stall

### Gliscor (BST 510, BW/XY/SM OU)
- **Stats:** 75/95/125/45/75/95
- **Combo:** Substitute + Toxic + Earthquake + Roost / Protect
- **Ability:** Poison Heal
- **Item:** Toxic Orb
- **Why it works:** Toxic Orb poisons Gliscor, but Poison Heal converts that into +1/8 HP per turn instead of damage. Substitute costs 25% HP but Poison Heal recoups it over 2 turns. Behind Sub, spreads Toxic on everything. Ground/Flying typing = Electric immune, Ground immune, Fighting resistant. Roost removes Flying type temporarily to dodge Ice/Rock.
- **Pattern:** Poison Heal + Toxic Orb + Sub = near-infinite sustain while spreading Toxic.

### Breloom (BST 410, BW OU)
- **Stats:** 60/130/80/60/60/70
- **Combo (Poison Heal):** Spore + Toxic Orb + Substitute + Focus Punch / Seed Bomb
- **Ability:** Poison Heal
- **Item:** Toxic Orb
- **Why it works:** Spore puts one threat to sleep, then Poison Heal Sub variant stalls. Focus Punch from behind Sub deals massive damage. Spore is 100% accurate sleep.
- **Combo (Technician):** Spore + Mach Punch + Bullet Seed + Low Sweep
- **Ability:** Technician
- **Why it works:** Technician boosts all three moves (all ≤60 BP) to 1.5x. Mach Punch becomes priority 60 BP. Bullet Seed = up to 150 BP. Spore still the crux — puts one mon to sleep for free momentum.
- **Pattern:** Spore (100% sleep) + any fighting STAB = always get a free turn.

## Magic Guard Stall/Offense

### Reuniclus (BST 490, BW OU)
- **Combo:** Magic Guard + Life Orb + Calm Mind + Psyshock
- **Why it works:** Magic Guard negates Life Orb recoil, hazard damage, Poison, Burn — all residual sources. Under Trick Room (30 base speed is slowest in tier), sweeps everything.
- **Pattern:** Magic Guard = free Life Orb (no recoil), free Toxic Orb (becomes usable for free status immunity), immune to hazards.

### Clefable (BST 483, XY+ OU)
- **Stats:** 95/70/73/95/90/60
- **Combo (Magic Guard):** Calm Mind + Moonblast + Flamethrower + Soft-Boiled
- **Ability:** Magic Guard
- **Item:** Life Orb
- **Why it works:** CM + Magic Guard Life Orb = no chip damage ever. Moonblast hits hard after boosts. Coverage hits Steel/Poison resists. Hard to KO due to natural bulk + CM + recovery.
- **Combo (Unaware):** Stealth Rock + Moonblast + Flamethrower + Soft-Boiled
- **Ability:** Unaware
- **Why it works:** Unaware ignores ALL opponent stat changes when calculating damage dealt AND received. Hard counter to every single setup sweeper in existence. Set up all you want — Clefable ignores it.
- **Pattern:** Unaware = absolute counter to all setup, regardless of +6 boosts.

## Regenerator Pivots/Walls

### Toxapex (BST 364, SM+ OU)
- **Stats:** 50/63/152/53/142/35
- **Combo:** Regenerator + Recover + Toxic Spikes + Scald + Haze
- **Ability:** Regenerator
- **Item:** Black Sludge
- **Why it works:** Regenerator heals 1/3 HP on switch out. Recover for in-battle healing. Haze removes all stat changes (hard resets opponent setup). 152 Def / 142 SpDef walls almost everything. Scald has 30% burn. Toxic Spikes poisons grounded switch-ins.
- **Pattern:** Regenerator + extreme bulk + Haze = unkillable utility that never needs to stay in.

### Slowbro/Slowking (BST 490, multiple gens OU)
- **Combo:** Regenerator + Scald + Slack Off + Thunder Wave + Toxic
- **Ability:** Regenerator
- **Item:** Leftovers
- **Why it works:** Free 33% heal on every switch. Scald burns. TWave paralyzes. Can't be worn down easily. Checks most physical attackers.
- **Pattern:** Regenerator + recovery move = effectively 1.5x HP pool.

### Tangrowth (BST 535, multiple gens OU)
- **Combo:** Regenerator + Knock Off + Leech Seed + Sleep Powder / Giga Drain
- **Ability:** Regenerator
- **Item:** Rocky Helmet / Assault Vest
- **Why it works:** Rocky Helmet punishes contact moves (1/6 HP damage). Regenerator on switch out. Leech Seed drains HP. Can wear down physical attackers just by existing.

## Iron Barbs / Rocky Helmet Contact Punishment

### Ferrothorn (BST 489, BW+ OU)
- **Stats:** 74/94/131/54/116/20
- **Combo:** Iron Barbs + Stealth Rock + Spikes + Leech Seed + Power Whip + Gyro Ball
- **Ability:** Iron Barbs
- **Item:** Rocky Helmet / Leftovers
- **Why it works:** Iron Barbs deals 1/8 damage on contact. Rocky Helmet adds another 1/6 on contact. Combined = nearly 1/3 HP on contact moves. Gyro Ball scales with speed difference (Ferrothorn base 20 Spe = massive Gyro Ball BP vs fast opponents). Leech Seed + Stealth Rock + Spikes = passive damage from all angles.
- **Pattern:** Iron Barbs + Rocky Helmet + Leech Seed = death by a thousand cuts to physical attackers.

## Physical Walls with Phazing

### Skarmory (BST 465, BW+ OU)
- **Stats:** 65/80/140/40/70/70
- **Combo:** Sturdy + Stealth Rock + Spikes + Whirlwind + Roost
- **Ability:** Sturdy
- **Item:** Leftovers
- **Why it works:** 140 Defense walls almost all physical attackers. Sturdy guarantees survival of one hit even from full. Whirlwind forces switches, racking up hazard damage. Roost for longevity. Best Spikes stacker in the game for many gens.
- **Pattern:** Physical wall + phazing + multi-layer hazards = chip damage factory.

### Hippowdon (BST 525, BW+ OU)
- **Stats:** 108/112/118/68/72/47
- **Combo:** Sand Stream + Stealth Rock + Earthquake + Slack Off + Whirlwind / Toxic
- **Ability:** Sand Stream
- **Item:** Smooth Rock / Leftovers
- **Why it works:** Sets permanent sand (until weather changes) dealing 6.25% HP/turn to non-rock/steel/ground types. Stealth Rock on top. Extreme physical bulk. Slack Off recovery. Forces passive damage on entire opposing team.
- **Pattern:** Sand Stream + passive chip = opponent's team slowly dies even without contact.

## Special Walls

### Chansey / Blissey (BST 450/540, multiple gens OU)
- **Chansey Stats:** 250/5/5/35/105/50 (with Eviolite: effectively 250/5/7/35/157/50)
- **Combo:** Eviolite + Natural Cure + Soft-Boiled + Stealth Rock + Seismic Toss + Thunder Wave
- **Ability:** Natural Cure (clears status on switch out)
- **Item:** Eviolite
- **Why it works:** Eviolite Chansey has higher special bulk than Blissey with better item slot. Natural Cure heals status on switch out. Seismic Toss deals 100 damage regardless of level. Counters every special attacker in the game.
- **Pattern:** Eviolite + Natural Cure + Seismic Toss = special wall with no weaknesses.

## Salt Cure / Passive Stall

### Garganacl (BST 500, SV OU)
- **Stats:** 100/100/130/45/90/35
- **Combo:** Salt Cure + Body Press + Recover + Stealth Rock / Stone Edge
- **Ability:** Purifying Salt (immune to all status conditions)
- **Item:** Leftovers / Heavy-Duty Boots
- **Why it works:** Salt Cure deals 1/8 HP per turn (1/4 to Water/Steel types). Purifying Salt = completely immune to Thunder Wave, Will-O-Wisp, Toxic. Body Press scales off Defense (130 base = strong Body Press). 130 Defense + 100 HP with Recover = unkillable wall.
- **Pattern:** Purifying Salt + Salt Cure = status immunity + passive damage, cannot be worn down by typical stall methods.

### Clodsire (BST 430, SV OU)
- **Stats:** 130/75/60/45/100/20
- **Combo:** Unaware + Recover + Toxic + Earthquake + Stealth Rock
- **Ability:** Unaware
- **Item:** Leftovers
- **Why it works:** Unaware ignores opponent boosts (same as Clefable). Massive HP + SpDef. Toxic + Recover = outlasts sweepers. Counters every special setup sweeper.
- **Pattern:** Unaware + Toxic + Recover = hard stop to all special setup.

### Dondozo (BST 530, SV OU)
- **Stats:** 150/100/115/65/65/35
- **Combo:** Unaware + Curse + Rest + Sleep Talk + Wave Crash
- **Ability:** Unaware
- **Item:** Leftovers
- **Why it works:** Unaware ignores opponent boosts so it walls boosted attackers. Curse stacks Defense and Atk at -Spe (already slow). After multiple Curses, hits hard and takes nothing. Rest restores HP.
- **Pattern:** Unaware + Curse = gets stronger while ignoring opponent's own setup.
