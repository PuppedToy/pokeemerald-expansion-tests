# Pivots & Support — Smogon Gen5-9 Research

Pokemon whose utility/pivot role made them tier staples above their raw stats.

---

## U-turn / Volt Switch Pivots

### Scizor (BST 430, BW+ OU)
- **Combo:** U-turn + Bullet Punch + Roost + Swords Dance
- **Ability:** Technician
- **Item:** Leftovers / Choice Band
- **Why it works:** U-turn generates momentum while Technician-boosted Bullet Punch is the strongest priority move in the tier. Incredibly hard to switch into — resists 11 of 18 types. Low BST but typing + ability makes it indispensable.
- **Pattern:** Technician + U-turn + priority = best of both momentum and offense.

### Rotom-W (BST 520, BW+ OU)
- **Stats:** 50/65/107/105/107/86
- **Combo:** Volt Switch + Will-O-Wisp + Scald + Pain Split
- **Ability:** Levitate
- **Item:** Leftovers / Choice Scarf
- **Why it works:** Levitate gives Ground immunity. Volt Switch generates momentum. Will-O-Wisp cripples physical attackers. Scald burns. Water/Electric STAB is nearly unresisted. The premier Volt-turn pivot for multiple gens.
- **Pattern:** Levitate + Volt Switch + status spreading = indispensable defensive pivot.

### Landorus-T (BST 580, BW+ OU)
- **Stats:** 89/145/90/105/80/91
- **Combo:** Stealth Rock + Earthquake + U-turn + Stone Edge / Knock Off
- **Ability:** Intimidate
- **Item:** Rocky Helmet / Choice Scarf
- **Why it works:** Intimidate halves opposing Attack on entry. U-turn escapes while generating momentum. Stealth Rock on the best rocker in the game. Intimidate + U-turn = free chip damage on physical attackers every time it comes in.
- **Pattern:** Intimidate + U-turn + Stealth Rock = utility trifecta. One Pokemon does three jobs.

### Tornadus-T (BST 580, BW OU)
- **Stats:** 79/100/80/110/90/121
- **Combo:** Regenerator + U-turn + Hurricane + Heat Wave / Focus Blast
- **Ability:** Regenerator
- **Item:** Choice Specs / Life Orb
- **Why it works:** Regenerator heals 33% on switch out. U-turn pivots while healing. Hurricane in rain (100% accuracy) hits hard. Fastest Regenerator user.
- **Pattern:** Regenerator + pivot move = net HP positive every switch cycle.

## Prankster Support

### Thundurus (BST 580, BW OU)
- **Combo:** Prankster + Thunder Wave + Taunt + Nasty Plot + Thunderbolt
- **Ability:** Prankster
- **Why it works:** Prankster gives +1 priority to non-damaging moves. Can paralyze faster threats before they move. Can Taunt walls before they Toxic/Stealth Rock. Can stop setup sweepers with priority Taunt.
- **Pattern:** Prankster + Thunder Wave = paralyzes anything regardless of speed.

### Whimsicott (BST 480, BW/XY OU fringe)
- **Combo:** Prankster + Encore + Leech Seed + Substitute / Stun Spore
- **Ability:** Prankster
- **Why it works:** Priority Encore locks opponent into a move (if they just used Swords Dance, they're stuck using SD). Priority Leech Seed drains HP. Hard to deal with as a lead.
- **Pattern:** Prankster + Encore = prevents opponent from ever switching out of a bad situation.

### Sableye (BST 380, ORAS+ OU)
- **Combo:** Prankster + Taunt + Will-O-Wisp + Recover + Foul Play
- **Ability:** Prankster
- **Item:** Leftovers
- **Why it works:** Priority Taunt stops all setup and stall. Priority Will-O-Wisp burns physical attackers before they move. Foul Play uses opponent's own Attack against them. Ghost/Dark typing = two immunities (Normal, Fighting).
- **Pattern:** Prankster + Taunt + Will-O-Wisp = controls tempo completely.

## Dual Screens Support

### Espeon (BST 525, BW/XY OU)
- **Stats:** 65/65/60/130/95/110
- **Combo:** Magic Bounce + Reflect + Light Screen + Baton Pass + Stored Power
- **Ability:** Magic Bounce
- **Item:** Light Clay
- **Why it works:** Magic Bounce reflects all entry hazards back at the user — opponents can't set up Stealth Rock against Espeon. Sets up dual screens for team. Baton Pass preserves screens momentum. Stored Power scales with boosts.
- **Pattern:** Magic Bounce + screens = denies hazard setup AND buffs entire team.

## Hazard Removal

### Excadrill (BST 508, BW+ OU) — Rapid Spin
- **Combo:** Rapid Spin + Stealth Rock + Earthquake + Iron Head
- **Ability:** Sand Rush / Mold Breaker
- **Why it works:** Best Rapid Spinner in sand (Sand Rush doubles speed making it impossible to outspeed). Clears hazards and sets its own.

### Corviknight (BST 495, SS+ OU)
- **Stats:** 98/87/105/53/85/67
- **Combo:** Mirror Armor + Defog + Roost + Brave Bird + U-turn
- **Ability:** Mirror Armor (reflects stat drops back to attacker)
- **Item:** Leftovers / Rocky Helmet
- **Why it works:** Mirror Armor reflects Intimidate back to the user (the opponent's Intimidate lowers THEIR attack instead). Defog removes hazards. Roost for longevity. Great typing (Flying/Steel = many resistances, immune to Poison).
- **Pattern:** Mirror Armor + Defog + Roost = hazard control that punishes Intimidate leads.

### Forretress (BST 465, multiple gens OU)
- **Combo:** Sturdy + Spikes + Rapid Spin + Volt Switch + Toxic Spikes
- **Ability:** Sturdy
- **Item:** Leftovers
- **Why it works:** Spinblocks (Ghost immunity via Steel...wait no). Sets multiple hazard layers, spins opponent's hazards, pivots with Volt Switch.

## Trapping Specialists

### Magnezone (BST 535, BW+ OU)
- **Stats:** 70/70/115/130/90/60
- **Combo:** Magnet Pull + Thunderbolt + Flash Cannon + Hidden Power Fire/Ice + Volt Switch
- **Ability:** Magnet Pull
- **Why it works:** Magnet Pull traps all Steel-type Pokemon. Removes Ferrothorn, Skarmory, Scizor from the game permanently. HP Fire obliterates trapped Steels. Opens up team's coverage dramatically.
- **Pattern:** Magnet Pull + HP Fire = eliminates the opponent's Steel-type wall for free.

## Trick Room Setters

### Reuniclus (BST 490, BW OU)
- **Combo:** Trick Room + Calm Mind + Psyshock + Focus Blast
- **Ability:** Magic Guard
- **Why it works:** Under TR, 30 base speed becomes an asset (moves last = moves first in TR). Magic Guard prevents chip. After CM, unkillable special tank.

### Cresselia (BST 600, slightly over)
- Classic TR setter but BST too high.

### Cofagrigus (BST 483, BW OU fringe)
- **Combo:** Trick Room + Nasty Plot + Shadow Ball + Hidden Power Fighting + Will-O-Wisp
- **Ability:** Mummy (contact attackers get Mummy ability, losing their own)
- **Why it works:** Mummy strips Speed Boost from Blaziken, Technician from Scizor, etc. TR setter that also punishes contact setups.
- **Pattern:** Mummy + Trick Room = strips key abilities from fast physical attackers.

## Weather Setters (Support Role)

### Politoed (BST 500, BW OU)
- **Stats:** 90/75/75/90/100/70
- **Combo:** Drizzle + Scald + Toxic + Encore + Perish Song
- **Ability:** Drizzle
- **Item:** Leftovers / Choice Specs
- **Why it works:** Permanent rain (until changed) boosts Water moves 50%, weakens Fire 50%, makes Thunder 100% accurate, and enables Swift Swim. Enables entire rain team archetype.
- **Pattern:** Drizzle = passive 50% buff to all Water moves for entire team.

### Ninetales (BST 505, BW OU)
- **Combo:** Drought + Will-O-Wisp + SolarBeam + Nasty Plot + Fire Blast
- **Ability:** Drought
- **Item:** Leftovers
- **Why it works:** Permanent sun boosts Fire moves 50%, weakens Water 50%, makes SolarBeam instant. Enables Chlorophyll sweepers.

### Hippowdon (BST 525, BW+ OU) — already in walls section
- Sand Stream enables Excadrill Sand Rush and passive chip.

### Tyranitar (BST 600, BW+ OU) — over BST threshold
- Sand Stream alternative to Hippowdon.

### Rillaboom (BST 530, SS OU)
- **Stats:** 100/125/90/60/70/85
- **Combo:** Grassy Surge + Grassy Glide + Swords Dance + High Horsepower + U-turn
- **Ability:** Grassy Surge
- **Item:** Choice Band / Life Orb
- **Why it works:** Grassy Surge sets Grassy Terrain, which gives +30% to Grass moves AND gives Grassy Glide +1 priority. Grassy Glide at +1 priority is like a strong priority move every turn in terrain. High Horsepower is not boosted by terrain (hits Rock/Steel/Fire neutrally). Terrain also heals all grounded pokemon 1/16 per turn.
- **Pattern:** Grassy Surge + Grassy Glide = terrain-enabled priority STAB every turn.

### Tapu Koko (BST 570, SM+ OU)
- **Stats:** 70/115/85/95/75/130
- **Combo:** Electric Surge + Thunderbolt/Thunder + Dazzling Gleam + U-turn + Roost
- **Ability:** Electric Surge
- **Item:** Choice Specs / Choice Scarf
- **Why it works:** Electric Surge sets Electric Terrain, boosting Electric moves 50% for grounded Pokemon. Combined with naturally high speed (130), hits extremely hard immediately. U-turn pivots. Misty/Psychic/Electric/Grassy Surge Tapus all dominated SM.
- **Pattern:** Electric Surge + fast pivot = immediate terrain pressure + momentum.
